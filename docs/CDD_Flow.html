CDD Department - Ticket Flow

Oliva Helpdesk | CDD to Clinic Manager → AOM → L2 Escalation Flow

Overview

Key Characteristics

  • CDD raises tickets for Clinic department
  • Auto-assigned to Center's Clinic Manager
  • Auto-escalation to AOM after 6 working hours
  • Auto-escalation to L2 after 14 working hours
  • CDD has special actions: Acknowledge, Reopen, Final Close
  • All client detail fields are mandatory

Key People

  • CDD User — Raises the ticket
  • Clinic Manager (CM) — First assignee per center
  • AOM — Area Operations Manager (L1 escalation)
  • L2 Manager — Final escalation level
  • CDD Admin — Admin access to CDD tickets

Complete Ticket Flow

STEP 1: CDD RAISES TICKET

CDD user fills all mandatory fields | Status: Open

Email: Raiser (Created) + Clinic Manager (Assigned)

▼

STEP 2: AUTO-ASSIGNED TO CLINIC MANAGER

System looks up Center → Clinic Manager Email → Auto-assigns

CM receives ticket and can: Acknowledge, Update Status, Resolve, Close

▼

CM RESOLVES

Resolved / Closed

Email: Raiser notified

CDD can Final Close or Reopen

CM DOES NOT RESOLVE

SLA: 6 working hours exceeded

Auto-escalates to AOM ▶

▼

STEP 3: AUTO-ESCALATED TO L1 (AOM)

Status: Escalated to L1 | Assigned to center's AOM

Email: AOM (Ticket Assigned)

AOM can: Resolve Close

▼

AOM RESOLVES

Resolved / Closed

Email: Raiser notified

CDD can Final Close or Reopen

AOM DOES NOT RESOLVE

SLA: 14 working hours (6+8)

Auto-escalates to L2 ▶

▼

STEP 4: AUTO-ESCALATED TO L2

Status: Escalated to L2 | Assigned to L2 Manager

Email: L2 Manager (Ticket Assigned)

▼

STEP 5: RESOLVED & CLOSED

L2 resolves the issue | Status: Resolved / Closed

Email: Raiser + Assignee

▼

STEP 6: CDD FINAL REVIEW

CDD reviews the resolution

Final Close — All queries addressed, ticket complete   OR   Reopen — Issue not fully resolved, back to assignee
