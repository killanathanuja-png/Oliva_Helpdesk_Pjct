Admin Department - Ticket Flow

Oliva Helpdesk | From Ticket Creation to Closure

Step 1: Raise a Ticket

Who Can Raise?

  • Helpdesk Admin users only
  • Each Helpdesk Admin belongs to a specific center
  • They raise tickets for their center's issues

What to Fill?

  • Department: Admin Department or IT Department
  • Main Category: e.g., FM Call, Electrical
  • Module: e.g., HELPDESK
  • Sub-Category: e.g., AC Not Working
  • Child Category: Specific issue type
  • Title & Description: Describe the issue
  • Priority: Critical / High / Medium / Low
Note: Center is auto-filled from the Helpdesk Admin's assigned center. No need to select manually.
Complete Ticket Flow

STEP 1: TICKET CREATED

Helpdesk Admin raises ticket | Status: Open

Email sent to: Raiser (Ticket Created)

▼

STEP 2: AUTO-ASSIGNED TO L1

System looks up Center → City → Escalation Matrix → L1 Person

Email sent to: L1 Assignee (Ticket Assigned)

▼

STEP 3: L1 WORKS ON TICKET

L1 person reviews, updates status, adds comments

Can set: In Progress Resolved Closed

▼

L1 RESOLVES

Resolved / Closed

Email: Raiser notified

TICKET COMPLETE

L1 DOES NOT RESOLVE

SLA exceeded: 8 working hours

Auto-escalates to L2 ▶

▼

STEP 4: AUTO-ESCALATED TO L2

Status: Escalated to L1 | Assigned to L2 person

Email sent to: L2 User (Ticket Assigned)

System comment: "Auto-escalated to L2. Originally assigned to [L1 name]"

▼

L2 RESOLVES

Resolved / Closed

Email: Raiser notified

TICKET COMPLETE

L2 DOES NOT RESOLVE

SLA exceeded: 18 working hours (8+10)

Auto-escalates to L3 ▶

▼

STEP 5: AUTO-ESCALATED TO L3

Status: Escalated to L2 | Assigned to L3 person

Email sent to: L3 User (Ticket Assigned)

System comment: "Auto-escalated to L3. L2 did not resolve."

▼

STEP 6: L3 RESOLVES & CLOSES

Resolved / Closed

Email sent to: Raiser + Assignee

TICKET COMPLETE
