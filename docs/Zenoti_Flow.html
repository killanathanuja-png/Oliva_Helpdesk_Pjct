Zenoti Department - Ticket Flow

Oliva Helpdesk | 3 Flows: Zenoti-Finance | Zenoti-Operational | Operational Issues

Overview

Key Characteristics

  • 3 distinct flows based on category type
  • Zenoti-Finance: AOM + Finance dual approval
  • Zenoti-Operational: AOM approval only
  • Operational Issues: No approval needed
  • Zenoti-specific fields (invoice, billing, client)
  • Priority auto-set: Finance = Critical, Others = Medium

Key People

  • Clinic Manager (CM) — Raises the ticket
  • AOM — First approver (auto-mapped by center)
  • Finance Team — Second approver (Zenoti-Finance only)
  • Zenoti Team — Works on approved tickets
  • Zenoti Team Manager — Oversees Zenoti tickets
