Quality & Audit Department - Ticket Flow

Oliva Helpdesk | From Ticket Creation to Closure

Overview

Key Characteristics

  • Simple workflow — no approval required
  • No auto-escalation — unlike Admin dept
  • No auto-assignment — goes to department
  • Category & Sub-Category are mandatory
  • Department aliases: "Quality & Audit" = "Quality"

Who Can Raise?

  • Any user who has access to Quality & Audit department
  • QA role users
  • Sr. Manager Quality & Audit role users
  • Other roles can also raise if department is available

Complete Ticket Flow

STEP 1: TICKET CREATED

User raises ticket for Quality & Audit | Status: Open

Email sent to: Raiser (Ticket Created)

▼

STEP 2: TICKET ASSIGNED

Ticket goes to Quality & Audit department

If assignee set: Email sent to Assignee (Ticket Assigned)

Center Manager also notified if applicable

▼

STEP 3: TEAM WORKS ON TICKET

Quality team reviews the issue, updates status, adds comments

Available: In Progress Follow Up

Email: Raiser + Assignee notified on every status change

▼

STEP 4: RESOLVED

Quality team resolves the issue | Status: Resolved

Email sent to: Raiser + Assignee (Status Update)

▼

STEP 5: CLOSED

Ticket is closed | Status: Closed

Email sent to: Raiser + Assignee (Status Update)

TICKET COMPLETE

Reopen: If the issue is not fully resolved, the raiser can reopen the ticket. Status changes to Reopened and the cycle repeats from Step 3.
